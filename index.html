Bootleg Gap

Trail Rehabilitation

Work is ongoing to refresh a few of the trails here at Bootleg.

For your own safety and the safety of our workers, please respect the trail closures. Hazards like heavy equipment and unfinished features may be present on closed trails at any time.
Also the reworked sections of trail are often in a sensative state. Riding them before they are ready causes damage and will further delay reopening.

We'll do our best to keep the closures to a minimum. This page will be kept up to date with what's going on.
